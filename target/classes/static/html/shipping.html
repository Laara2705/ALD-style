Shipping


At ALD Shoes, our goal is to make shoe shopping as easy and convenient as possible for our customers. That's why we offer a clear and simple shipping policy.

Delivery times: We promise to deliver your shoes within 3 to 7 business days after your order is confirmed. If there is any delay in processing your order, we will contact you as soon as possible to inform you of any issues and provide a new delivery date.

Shipping cost: The shipping cost is calculated based on the weight of your order and the shipping address. Shipping cost details will be provided to you when you place your order. We offer free shipping on orders over $100.

Packaging: We make sure that your shoes arrive in perfect condition, so we send them in a strong and secure box.

Shipping tracking: Once your order has been shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number so that you can track your package and always know where your order is.

Returns and Exchanges: If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day return and exchange policy from the date of delivery. See our return and exchange policy for more information.

In short, at ALD Shoes we strive to provide an exceptional online shopping experience, and our commitment to delivering quality shoes and customer satisfaction is a key part of that.
